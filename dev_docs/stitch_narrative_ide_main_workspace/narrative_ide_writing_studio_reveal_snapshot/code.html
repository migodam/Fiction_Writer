The Hideout

The rain hammered against the rusted corrugated roof, drowning out the distant sirens. @Elara dropped her pack on the concrete floor, the heavy thud barely audible over the storm. She leaned against the cold brick wall, catching her breath.

"We're clear," @Jax said, stepping out from the shadows near the boarded-up window. He held the Quantum Drive loosely in his left hand, its faint blue glow casting long, strange shadows across his face.

Elara nodded, pushing wet hair out of her eyes. "Did @Vane see you grab it?"

Jax hesitated. It was a fractional pause, barely noticeable, but Elara had spent years reading his tells. He tightened his grip on the drive. "No. He was busy dealing with the perimeter guards."

She didn't believe him. If Vane knew they had the drive, they wouldn't just be running from the authorities—they'd have the Syndicate on them too.
