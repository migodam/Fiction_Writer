Scene 1: Prologue

The rain lashed against the cobblestone streets of Oakhaven, washing away the grime but leaving a profound sense of despair in its wake. Elara pulled her cloak tighter, her breath pluming in the frigid air. The tavern's sign, a battered oak leaf swinging precariously on a rusty hinge, offered the only solace in the encroaching gloom.

She pushed open the heavy wooden door, greeted by a wave of stale ale and woodsmoke. The murmur of conversations halted momentarily, eyes assessing the newcomer before returning to their murky depths. Elara ignored the stares, making her way to a secluded corner booth.

"He said he'd be here," she muttered to herself, tracing the rim of an empty mug left by a previous patron.

Before she could signal the barkeep, a shadow detached itself from the gloom across the room, gliding silently towards her table. The hood obscured their face, but the subtle glint of steel beneath their cloak was unmistakable.
