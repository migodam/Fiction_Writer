The Quantum Protocol - Act 1

Elaria stood before the primary console, the soft hum of the quantum drive vibrating through the deck plates. The chronometer blinked, a relentless countdown to standard orbital insertion.

She tapped the display, bringing up the localized sensor array. "Kael, are you seeing this interference pattern on the dark matter bands?"

Kael leaned over from the adjacent station, his brow furrowed. "It's chaotic. Doesn't match any known natural phenomena. It almost looks... intentional."

A sudden jolt rocked the ship, throwing them both off balance. Red warning lights strobed across the bridge, casting long, frantic shadows.

"Hull breach in sector four!" Kael shouted over the blaring klaxons.

Elaria gritted her teeth, her hands flying across the controls to seal the bulkheads. "This isn't just interference. We're under attack."
